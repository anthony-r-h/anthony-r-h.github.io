# About

This started as a headband project and turned into a full custom build.

![Custom headphone build, angle view](/files/GHEADBAND100/1/post/headband-1.jpg)

## What Changed

- Driver: Turbulent Labs X Driver (@turbulentlabs)
- Ear cups: custom blackened walnut cups with modular cable setup by Bruce Mackenzie (@wabi_sabi_headphones)
- Gimbals and rod blocks: Nyczaj
- Headband: Me

## Headband

![Pattern](/files/GHEADBAND100/pattern.png)

Outer is Badalassi Carlo waxy pull-up veg-tan leather. The headband is padded and finished with a rolled edge.

![Custom headphone build, front detail](/files/GHEADBAND100/1/post/headband-2.jpg)

The liner side is a soft goatskin, padded with thick neoprene foam and also finished with a rolled edge.

![Custom headphone build, side detail](/files/GHEADBAND100/1/post/headband1-1.jpg)
